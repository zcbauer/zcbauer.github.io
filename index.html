My Projects

    Projects
    Smart Scoreboard September - December 2023
    I did this as my second Senior Capstonefor EECS 473:Advanced Embedded Systems. My contributions where designing and assembling a PCBs for 2 wearable controllers. Additionally, I created a protocol for sending commands to the scoreboard wirelessly from the controllers that I built. I also created a menu system that was used for a touchscreen controller on the board itself.
    BMS System Testing Apparatus May - July 2023
    Completed while I was an intern for the Ford Motor Company. I designed and built a system to test the BMS for Ford vehicles. While working on the project, I cultivated knowledge in analog signal processing.
    Skin Touch Tracking Device February - April 2023
    Senior Capstone project. Done for my Human-Computer Interaction course. The device I helped design was able to detect and localize a person's finger when they touched their arm. An oscillator that I built created a 60 MHz electrical signal that was transmitted through the skin.
